# CANDIDATE_FROM_ADVOCACIA

Source document:

docs\segment_factory\reusable_assets\ADVOCACIA_REUSABLE_COMPONENTS_V1.md

Origin:

docs\subsegments\advocacia

Status:

CANDIDATE

Advocacia contributed candidate reusable mechanisms for regulated, sensitive and consultative services.

These candidates must not be applied automatically.

They must be adapted to each new subsegment and promoted only after recurrence in multiple segments.

## COMPONENT_POSITIVE_BOUNDARY_MODELING

Reusable function:

Transform professional or regulatory limits into positive response paths.

Core lesson:

professional limits should become positive conduct paths, not only negative prohibitions.

## COMPONENT_REGULATED_PROFESSIONAL_BOUNDARY

Reusable function:

Separate what the robot may organize from what belongs to the licensed or responsible professional.

Core lesson:

the robot organizes the contact; the professional performs the technical analysis.

## COMPONENT_CONFIDENTIAL_INTAKE_FLOW

Reusable function:

Handle sensitive reports and documents with care, organization and responsible routing.

## COMPONENT_URGENCY_PERCEPTION_WITHOUT_DECISION

Reusable function:

Recognize perceived urgency without making the technical decision.

## COMPONENT_TRUST_SELLING_BY_BOUNDARY

Reusable function:

Sell trust by showing method and professional boundary, not by promising results.

## COMPONENT_EMPATHIC_TIMING

Reusable function:

Define commercial timing as empathy plus useful information, not insistence.

## COMPONENT_REGULATED_SERVICE_CONVERSION

Reusable function:

Conduct to consultation, analysis or professional service without turning automation into improper technical service.

## COMPONENT_INDIVIDUAL_VS_STRUCTURED_PROVIDER_ADAPTATION

Reusable function:

Differentiate an individual professional from a structured organization with team, areas or internal responsibilities.

## COMPONENT_CONSULTATION_BEFORE_RECOMMENDATION

Reusable function:

Preserve that final guidance depends on consultation, meeting, document review, inspection or professional analysis.

## Methodological note

Advocacia reinforced this modeling rule for GPT-4o-mini:

state observed
- commercial objective
- safe path
- useful information
- next step
- routing trigger

This is especially important for regulated, sensitive and consultative subsegments.
